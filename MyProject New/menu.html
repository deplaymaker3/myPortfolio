MENU RESERVATIONS ABOUT US LOCATIONS


The Bar Room Menu

The lively Bar Room offers vividly seasonal contemporary cooking à la carte. We welcome walk-ins as well as reservations up to 28 days in advance.

DownTown's wine list includes 3,000 selections from around the world, balancing classics with emerging producers to suit all tastes. Since 2016 DownTown has held a Grand Award from Wine Spectator.
DownTown Lounge offers a carefully curated program of cocktails, beer, and spirits. Cutting-edge or classic, we have something for every taste.






The Kitchen Table

The Kitchen Table offers a front-row seat in the heart of the restaurant’s kitchen for up to four guests, with an inventive, multi-course tasting menu. From this exclusive perch, you can take in the buzzing activity of DownTown's world-class kitchen while your dining experience unfolds before your eyes.

Tasting menu prices are per person.
